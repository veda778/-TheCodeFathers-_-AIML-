Company Listings

Software Companies

Microsoft

Microsoft is a leading global vendor of computer software, hardware for computer systems, and cloud services.

Apple

Apple designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories worldwide.

Google

Google specializes in Internet-related services and products, which include online advertising technologies, a search engine, and cloud computing.

Adobe

Adobe is known for its multimedia and creativity software products, with a more recent foray towards digital marketing software.

Oracle

Oracle provides database software and technology, cloud engineered systems, and enterprise software products.

IBM

IBM produces and sells computer hardware, middleware, and software, and provides hosting and consulting services.

Hardware Companies

Intel

Intel is a leading manufacturer of semiconductor chips, microprocessors, and other hardware components.

AMD

AMD is known for its computer processors and related technologies for business and consumer markets.

NVIDIA

NVIDIA designs graphics processing units (GPUs) for the gaming and professional markets, as well as system on a chip units (SoCs) for the mobile computing and automotive market.

Dell

Dell is a multinational computer technology company that develops, sells, repairs, and supports computers and related products and services.

HP

HP develops and provides a wide variety of hardware components as well as software and related services to consumers, small- and medium-sized businesses, and large enterprises.

Lenovo

Lenovo designs, develops, manufactures, and sells personal computers, tablet computers, smartphones, workstations, servers, electronic storage devices, IT management software, and smart televisions.

Other Domains

Tesla

Tesla specializes in electric vehicles, battery energy storage from home to grid scale, solar panels, and solar roof tiles.

SpaceX

SpaceX designs, manufactures, and launches advanced rockets and spacecraft with the goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars.

Amazon

Amazon focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence.

Samsung

Samsung is a South Korean multinational conglomerate that produces a wide range of electronics and appliances, including smartphones, televisions, and home appliances.

Sony

Sony is a Japanese multinational conglomerate corporation that produces electronics, gaming, entertainment, and financial services.

Siemens

Siemens is a global powerhouse focusing on the areas of electrification, automation, and digitalization.
